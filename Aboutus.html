About Us

Our History

PPM Utility Management Solutions (Prepay Metering CC) was established by Johan Bester in 2006. The family business has grown from the first prepaid electricity meters managed in Hermanus, to building relationships with more than 200 clients located across the Cape Peninsula and beyond.

PPM strives to provide an all-inclusive function and service to Developers, Home Owners Associations (HOA’s), Body Corporates (BC’s) and their representatives. We believe in a no-nonsence approach with transparent and simplified processes to empower the Body Corporate with the necessary information and tools to make informed decisions. Our main focus is convenience for the end-consumer (owner, resident and/or tenant) with the emphasis on support and guidance in an environment where it is easy for the owner, resident and/or tenant to feel overwhelmed. We believe in buidling lasting relationships and not just selling utilities to a customer.
